Who is Kim?

Hi there, my name is Kimberly Small, but you can call me Kim. I am a full-time student at Coastal Carolina University (CCU) majoring in Computer Science, while also working part-time as an IT Assistant for Student Computing Services at CCU. I plan on graduating in May of 2018, then beginning my full-time career as a developer.

"Creativity is the way I share my soul with the world." - Brene Brown

Creativity plays a MAJOR role in my everyday life. I enjoy listening to all types of music, expanding my palate while trying new recipes - I LOVE pizza by the way, pinning fresh and new ideas on Pinterest, and exploring the exciting world of Computer Science. I also enjoy exercising and creating a healthier life style, talking long walks, binge watching on Netflix and Hulu, and exploring new worlds and ideas through reading.

I have an interest in development, both web and software, and also have an interest in UX/UI design. I guess you could say I have a preference for front-end development, but I can also perform back-end development as well. Additionally, I am trying to build my experience with Graphic Design. I have high hopes that one day, I will be able to do it all and become a Full-Stack Developer!

"To be creative is to let little pieces of your heart go, & place them into each project you make." - Pat Bravo

With experience ranging from making awesome tacos at Taco Bell for years to collaborating in a team of 3 students to create a help desk ticket web application, I aspire to design and construct beautiful applications with the user in mind. I am also open to designing anything from flyers and business cards, to icons and web graphics. Overall, I just want to CREATE, so let's create something magnificent together!
